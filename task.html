Make a perfect cup of tea with just a few steps!

Ingredients



1 cup water

1 tea bag
1 teaspoon of sugar(optional)
1/2 cup milk(optional)
slice of lemon(optional)


Instructions

1.Boil water
2.place tea bag in a cup.
3.pour hot water over the tea.
4.Steep for3-5mintues.
5.Add sugar or honey if you like.
6.Add milk if you prefer.
7.Add lemon if desired.
8.Enjoy your tea!
